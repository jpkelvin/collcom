Our Social Projects

We plan, design and assess different community engagement projects. We provide a platform for youth and corporate to work closely with community/villages such as street children, migrated people, slums, orphanage, old-age, safai-karamchari, divyang, drugs abused people, jail inmates, farmers and so on to understand their problems, needs, and involve themselves in the problem solving process, so that life of these people or villagers can be raised at higher material and moral level.

    Joy of Sharing

We collect the old clothes from students and family and distribute to the near by slum area and ran basera. We also distrubute toys and stationary materials to the children of slum basti.
